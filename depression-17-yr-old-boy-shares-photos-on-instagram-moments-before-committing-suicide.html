17-yr-old boy shares photos on Instagram moments before committing suicide

And moments later, he had taken a dive into the Detroit River. His body was recovered few hours later.

The young chap who seemed very happy with life, going by his photos, posted the shocking messages giving his friends timely directions that would lead to the end of his life.

He started by posting a photo and a message of himself where he wrote "I'm here", giving an indication that he had gotten to the destination where he would commit the suicide.
