Offset and Quavo Reunite At BET Awards in Surprise Performance, Pay Tribute to Take Off

  • Offset and Quavo of Migos reunited on stage at the 2023 BET Awards, marking their first performance together since the tragic death of Takeoff
  • The duo paid tribute to Takeoff with a heartfelt performance, featuring his verse from "Hotel Lobby" and a visual display honoring his memory
  • The unannounced performance and tribute left a lasting impact, showcasing the enduring bond between the Migos members

Offset and Quavo, two members of the hip-hop group Migos, reunited on stage to perform and pay tribute to their late bandmate Takeoff.

Surprise reunion

The Sunday, June 25, performance began with a surprise appearance by Offset and Quavo, who walked into a spotlight surrounded by smoke and lasers.

According to Billboard, Takeoff's verse from "Hotel Lobby," a song from their 2022 spin-off project, played as they danced.

Emotional tribute

The duo then performed "Bad and Boujee," Migos' hit song that reached No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart in 2017.

This marked the first performance for Offset and Quavo together since Takeoff's death.

It's worth noting that Offset did not perform with Migos during their festival dates the previous year, so this was their first time on stage together in at least a year.

Offset's wife, Cardi B, expressed her appreciation for the moment on Twitter, stating that she was proud of the boys.

The 2023 BET Awards continued with other performances and presentations, including a lifetime achievement award presentation to Busta Rhymes.

Takeoff was killed by stray bullet

In November 2022, the manager of Migos attributed the death of Takeoff to a stray bullet.

Takeoff, 28, was shot dead after an altercation at a gaming facility in Houston, Texas after an altercation.

According to Daily Mail, dramatic footage captured the moment Takeoff and his uncle Quavo engaged in an argument before the shot rang out.

The rapper was pronounced dead at the scene after the ill-fated shot struck him in his upper body.

Quality Control Music said in:

"It is with broken hearts and deep sadness that we confirm the loss of our beloved brother Kirsnick Khari Ball, known to the world as Takeoff. Senseless violence and a stray bullet has taken another life from this world and we are devastated."
