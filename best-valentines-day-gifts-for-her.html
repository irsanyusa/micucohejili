Best Valentine's Gifts for Her: 38 Ideas for Every Budget

Valentine’s Day is the perfect time to show affection to your loved one. Homemade cards, candy hearts, sweet-smelling flowers, and sexy lingerie are all on Cupid’s greatest hits list, but there are endless ways to say “I love you” with the perfect gift for her. And sometimes, thinking outside the (chocolate) box is the best way to delight your darling Valentine this year.

What to consider when choosing a Valentine's Day Gift for her

Any kind of gift giving should take into account the individual receiving it. Whether you are shopping for your Valentine's or a birthday present, the key to great gift-giving is carefully considering the recipient and what they love, their hobbies, interests, and personality. If you are in a new relationship, take the time to ask subtle questions or make careful observations when you are together. Gift giving should be fun, not stressful, so here are some things to consider when Valentine’s Day shopping for the best gifts for women.

Think outside the (gift) box

On Valentine’s Day, it is easy to grab some chocolates and roses and call it a day, and while there is nothing wrong with tradition, taking the time to think about the recipient, do they love houseplants over fresh-cut flowers or prefer licorice over bon-bons, can help you choose a thoughtful gift. If you are giving a gift to someone you don’t know well, start with what you have in common. For example, maybe you both enjoy going to the movies (consider tickets to a new show) or hiking (cute wool socks), and you can build gifts around that. Or consider giving a gift that would be meaningful to you like a copy of your favorite book. Take whatever details you know about a person and use your imagination.

Consider the individual

Valentine’s Day often brings champagne, rose petals, and chocolates to mind, but make sure you are tailoring your gifts to the individual. You wouldn’t want to give someone sober a bottle of wine, or offer a box of truffles to someone allergic to dairy. Don’t be afraid to ask someone you may be dating but not know very well outright if they have allergies or similar health issues. If you know nothing is off limits, consider what the recipient loves, from their hobbies to their favorite flowers. And check sizes before you purchase clothing or lingerie.

Set a budget or limit on your gifts

Don’t go broke over Valentine’s Day when a thoughtful homemade cardcan go a long way. You can find ways to show your affection without maxing out your Amex, and if you don’t know someone well, buying an expensive gift runs the risk of creating an awkward situation. Small gifts with sentimental value may be a safer route to take, but if you want to splurge, discussing a price range with your partner, spouse, or girlfriend can be a good way to set some spending limits without ruining a surprise.

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

When is Valentine's Day 2024?

Valentine’s Day always falls on February 14 and in 2024, it falls on a Wednesday, which will liven up your midweek.

What do you get a woman for Valentine's Day?

It depends on the woman! Some women love romantic gifts, while others like spicier options; some women like flowers, and others are allergic. Think about the individual - what they like and dislike, and their hobbies and interests, and buy a gift that considers who they are and what they love.

What is a good Valentine’s gift for girlfriends?

When shopping for a gift for your girlfriend, whether a new relationship or a long-time love, you should take some time to reflect on what makes her special. Maybe she loves to travel or can’t resist a great board game. Think about what she loves and enjoys and go from there. If you are really stumped, flowers and chocolate are classics for a reason.

What are the three most popular Valentine's Day gifts?

According to 2023 research by the National Retailers Federation (NRF), the top three gifts consumers planned to give for Valentine’s Day were, in this order, chocolate, greeting cards, and flowers, with an evening out and jewelry coming in fourth and fifth as top gifts.

What is the best gift for your wife on Valentine's Day?

The best gift to give your wife on Valentine’s Day depends on who you are married to. There isn’t a one-size-fits-all present to please all wives. Think about what she likes, her hobbies, her passions, and what gifts are meaningful to her before you buy, and remember, it’s not the gift. It's the thoughtfulness and love behind it that really matters.

What’s a Galentine?

Valentines for your friends, often called Galentines, are a great way to show your platonic pals how much they mean to you. Traditional gifts of flowers and chocolates will likely be appreciated, but beautiful paper cards, bottles of wine, or even a handmade coupon book of “things to do together” like a movie night, winter hike, or an at-home wine tasting for your best friends, are all suggestions for relatively inexpensive ways to say “thank you for being a friend” on Valentine’s Day.
