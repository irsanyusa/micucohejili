The Masked Singer host Nick Cannon is officially the father of 12 children, as model Alyssa Scott just announced she gave birth to their second child together. Earlier this month, the pair welcomed a daughter named Halo Marie Cannon. Scott shared a video from the baby’s birth on Instagram.

Nick Cannon, Alyssa Scott Welcome Baby Daughter

Cannon and Scott previously welcomed a son named Zen in 2021. Sadly, Zen passed away of brain cancer at 5 months old. The pair announced earlier this year that they were expecting another child together. On December 14, Scott gave birth.

Scott shared a video on Thursday showing the moment her newborn daughter was born. Cannon was there in the delivery room, and he told Scott that it was a girl. Scott can be seen holding her baby in an emotional moment.

“Zen is in every breath I take. I know his spirit was with us in the room that morning. I know he is watching down on us,” Scott wrote in the video’s caption, adding, “I will hold onto this memory forever.”

Halo is Scott’s third child; she also has a daughter named Zeela from another relationship. As Scott wrote of Halo, “I will remember the sound of her crying out with her first breath and feeling her heartbeat against mine. My sweet girl, I got my surprise!!”

This Is Cannon’s 12th Child

With the birth of Halo, Nick Cannon has become a father 12 times over, with six different women. This year alone, he’s welcomed five children with five women — three girls and two boys.

In June, Bre Tiesi gave birth to their first child together, a son named Legendary Love. In September, he also welcomed his first child with LaNisha Cole, a daughter named Onyx Ice. That same month, Brittany Bell gave birth to their third child together, a son named Rise Messiah.

Then in November, Cannon welcomed his third child with Abby De La Rosa, a daughter named Beautiful Zeppelin. The star also has a daughter and a son named Monroe and Moroccan with his ex-wife Mariah Carey.
