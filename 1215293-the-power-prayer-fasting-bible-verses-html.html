The power of prayer and fasting: Bible verses

What is the power of prayer and fasting? Prayer is the most powerful force in the universe that a believing Christian can use. Blessed progress will manifest itself in the Assembly of God depending on how many Christians pray and fast!

Every believer feels the benefits of fasting and prayer. But if you need a prove from Holy Scripture, read on and find all the answers you are looking for.

  • John 14:14: If you ask me anything in my name, I will do it.

  • Mark 11:24: Therefore I tell you, whatever you ask in prayer, believe that you have received it, and it will be yours.

Give us this day our daily bread, and forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors. And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil.
