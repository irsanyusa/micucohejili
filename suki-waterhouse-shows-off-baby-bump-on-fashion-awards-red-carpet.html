Suki Waterhouse showed off her baby bump on the red carpet on Monday for the 2023 Fashion Awards in London. Waterhouse recently confirmed that she and her partner Robert Pattinson are expecting their first child together.

Suki Waterhouse Shows Baby Bump on Red Carpet

The 2023 Fashion Awards were held Monday at the Royal Albert Hall in London. Waterhouse attended the event in a red strapless red gown from the H&M Studio Holiday Capsule. The dress had a slit up the front that showed off her legs.

Waterhouse cradled her baby bump in photos from the red carpet. Her pregnant belly was accented by the gown’s ruffle detail. The actress and singer accessorized the look with dark red platform heels and simple jewelry.

Waterhouse confirmed her pregnancy last month during a performance at the Corona Capital Festival in Mexico. In a video taken by a fan, Waterhouse can be heard telling the crowd that she wore a sparkly outfit to “distract you from something else that’s going on,” referring to her baby bump.

Recently, Waterhouse released a cover of “Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas,” with a portion of the proceeds going to the charity A Sense of Home.

Waterhouse, Pattinson Have Been Together Since 2018

Waterhouse and Pattinson have been dating for over five years, having first been linked in July 2018. They started living together in London during the Covid-19 pandemic. Interestingly, they didn’t make their red carpet debut together until December 2022.

“I’m shocked that I’m so happy with someone for nearly five years,” Waterhouse said in an interview with The Times, adding of Pattinson, “We’ve always got so much to say, and I find him hilarious.”

A source told People last month that Waterhouse and Pattinson are “thrilled beyond words” to be expecting their first child together. Another source added that they’re “looking forward to becoming parents.”
