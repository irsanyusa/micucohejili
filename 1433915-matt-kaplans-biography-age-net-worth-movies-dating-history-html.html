Matthew Kaplan's biography

How old is Matt Kaplan? The film producer was born on April 14, 1984, in California, USA. This makes him 38 years old as of 2022. Where does Matt Kaplan live? He currently resides in Los Angeles, California.

Shortly after completing high school, Kaplan enrolled at Columbia University and graduated with a bachelor's degree in Film Studies. This marked the beginning of his decorated film and TV production career.

What does Matt Kaplan do for a living?

Matt ventured into the entertainment industry in the late 1990s. He began by working as an intern at the United Talent Agency and the Todd Phillips Company. His entrepreneurial journey began when he founded the One Films company in 2013.

This was followed by his appointment to the president position at Awesomeness TV in 2017. A few months later, he founded a company known as ACE Entertainment and began producing films and television shows in-house.
