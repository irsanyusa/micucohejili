Food Tiles Match 3

About “Food Tiles Match 3”

This is a fun and exciting match 3 game. You need to match 3 or more tiles in the same row or column to collect them. Collect all the required tiles in each level to complete it. The game offers 96 levels to play, and the global leaderboard is supported. Many special items and cool effects are waiting for you!

How to play “Food Tales Match 3”

Tap and swipe to switch the tiles. You need to match 3 or more tiles in the same row or column to collect them.
