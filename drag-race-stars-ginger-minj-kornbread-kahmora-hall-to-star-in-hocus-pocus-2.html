RuPaul’s Drag Race favorites Ginger Minj, Kornbread, and Kahmorah Hall have joined the cast of the upcoming Hocus Pocus 2 movie. The three queens will portray the drag versions of the infamous Sanderson Sisters in the sequel to the classic 1993 film.

Season 7 contestant Ginger Minj will portray the drag version of Bette Midler’s character, Winifred Sanderson. Season 13 Drag Race alum Kahmorah will play the drag version of Sarah Jessica Parker’s character, Sarah Sanderson. Season 14’s Kornbread will play the drag version of Mary Sanderson, played by Kathy Najimy.

The Disney+ sequel filmed this past November in Rhode Island. The three queens apparently perform as the sisters in a drag show when the real witches when they make their return.

‘Hocus Pocus 2’ Will Premiere in Time for Halloween

In Hocus Pocus 2, three young women accidentally bring back the Sanderson Sisters to modern day Salem and must figure out how to stop the child-hungry witches from wreaking havoc on the world. In addition to the Drag Race stars, most of the original cast is set to return for the new movie.

“I am beyond thrilled to be involved in the sequel to a film that is as beloved as Hocus Pocus,” director Anne Fletcher said in a statement. “Fans around the world have embraced these characters and have made this film a Halloween tradition whose popularity continues to grow, and how lucky am I to be back at Disney with these three extraordinarily talented ladies in the iconic roles they created, as well as our fabulous new additions to the cast?”

Hocus Pocus 2 is scheduled to premiere on Disney+ this fall, just in time for Halloween. Parker, Middler, and Najimy’s iconic roles have been impersonated by drag queens since the early 2000s. It was only right to make the audience’s dreams come true with the upcoming plot twist.
