'America's Got Talent' Vegas Live Shows Re-Brands to 'Superstars Live!'

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

America’s Got Talent Presents SUPERSTARS Live via Instagram

Reading Time: 3 minutes

The America’s Got Talent live show in Las Vegas recently presented an all new brand name, Superstars Live. Eight highly exciting acts from previous AGT seasons will continue to wow the live audience on this 75-minute show.

America’s Got Talent Presents Superstars Live At The Luxor Hotel

This fast-paced, high-action variety show offers show-stopping moments ranging from thrill acts and magicians to vocalists and acrobats. All acts are performed by past AGT stars, in honor of some of the finest alums from the hit franchise.

The Luxor hotel just got a bigger and better show starting December 21, and continues every night to January. Hosted by AGT season 17 finalist and comedian Mike E. Winfield, the show typically starts at 6:00pm ending after 75 minutes of jaw dropping performances.

Tickets for the live shows are selling out fast, with most of the tickets for the upcoming nights being 80 percent sold already. Ticket prices for the live shows range from $61 to $105, fans can buy tickets at the official website of the Luxor Hotel.

The Eight Acts Performing at Superstars Live

1. Kodi Lee

The winner of season 14 of AGT, and an incredibly talented singer and pianist despite being blind and autistic. He continues to grace the audiences with his unbelievable singing prowess that is truly one of a kind.

2. Aidan Bryant

The aerialist and runner-up of season 16 of AGT, Aidan Bryant delivers death-defying performances that leave audiences at the edge of their seats.

3. Light Balance

Light Balance is a light-up dance group that utilizes neon light clothing during their mesmerizing dance routines. Their high-energy routine can really get the crowd standing and being left amazed at what they just witnessed. The group are finalists from season 12 of AGT.

4. The Pack Drumline

The drum and percussion group from AGT season 17 makes their highly anticipated stage performance on the vegas live shows.

5. Celia Muñoz

Celia Muñoz is the ventriloquist that rarely uses a puppet but instead makes entertaining singing and acting her forte. She is a finalist from season 17 of AGT, and has performed with the likes of Darci Lynne and Terry Fator.

6. Deadly Games

This iconic AGT group began with now estranged couple Alfredo and Anna Silva. Together, the former couple competed on AGT season 11, as well as AGT: The Champions. In present day, Alfredo Silva performs with his new girlfriend, Aleksandra Kiedrowicz. Combining daring knife throwing with jaw dropping stunts, Deadly Games will surely leave everyone’s jaws hanging.

7. The Silhouettes

True to their name, the group performs a shadow play dance by creating unforgettable stories with their silhouettes. They competed in season six of AGT, finishing at second place.

8. Mervant Vera

Mervant Vera is a magician and rapper from season 17 of AGT, he finished at 3rd place on the show. This unique performer is set to compete in the upcoming season of AGT All-Stars.

We expect the line up to be everchanging for this show, since there are so many amazing super stars that have rose to fame from AGT. Stay tuned into the show’s social media for more performer announcements.
