What Are 'America's Got Talent' 2022 Winners, The Mayyas, Up To Now?

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

After their sensational win on America’s Got Talent Season 17, the Lebanese dance troupe, The Mayyas, took the world by storm with their breathtaking performances. Led by founder and choreographer Nadim Cherfan, the group captivated the hearts of judges and viewers alike. In this article, we will explore what The Mayyas have been up to since their AGT win and shed light on their future plans.

The Mayyas Have Been Performing Worldwide

The Mayyas, a Lebanese dance company, won Season 17 of AGT, securing the $1 million prize and the opportunity to headline a show a the Luxor in Las Vegas. They made their AGT debut in June and impressed judges Simon Cowell, Heidi Klum, Howie Mandel, and Sofia Vergara, ultimately receiving Vergara’s golden buzzer and a fast track to the live shows.

The impact of Mayyas’ win extended beyond AGT. They shared a special bond with judge Sofia Vergara, who has Colombian-Lebanese roots. The group honored her by awarding her one of their necklaces, symbolizing the Lebanese cedar tree. Vergara then expressed her appreciation for the gift and its significance to her cultural heritage.

Following their AGT triumph, Mayyas traveled to Saudi Arabia’s capital, Riyadh, to mesmerize the crowd once again. Taking center stage at Riyadh Season’s Boulevard World on November 21 and 22, Mayyas left the audience stunned with their awe-inspiring performance. This marked their first regional show outside of Lebanon, solidifying their growing international presence further.

Mayyas’ remarkable journey continued as they then took the stage alongside global icon Beyoncé in a private event held to celebrate the opening of Atlantis The Royal in Dubai. During the event, Mayyas joined Beyoncé on stage, captivating the audience with their brilliant dance routines.

With their unwavering dedication and commitment to their craft, the Mayyas are set to explore new creative avenues, pushing boundaries and bringing their innovative dance routines to even more stages worldwide.

Lebanese Celebrities and Fans Continue to Praise and Support Mayyas’ Success

The admiration and support for the Mayyas, the talented Lebanese dance group, remain strong even after their remarkable victory on AGT. Lebanese people, including celebrities, continue to celebrate their achievements, uplifting spirits in a country that has faced immense challenges.

Fans from all walks of life, regardless of age or religious background, are filled with pride and joy for the Mayyas’ mesmerizing performances. Social media platforms have been buzzing with heartfelt messages of support.

As the Mayyas’ journey continues, Lebanese people remain steadfast in their support and admiration. The group’s triumph serves as an inspiration, reminding the nation of the resilience and extraordinary talent that resides within its borders.
