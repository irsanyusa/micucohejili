Kwara State University courses offered and cut off mark in 2024/2025

Kwara State University, also known as KWASU, is a fully registered and accredited university in Nigeria. It aims to offer high-quality education to all students. Numerous Nigerian youth have graduated from various faculties, and many others are enrolled on multiple academic programmes.

Nigeria has many universities located in different parts of the country, and Kwara State University is one of them. Many people are curious to see the list of Kwara State University courses to establish if it is the right institution for them. Read on to learn all the courses offered and the entry requirements.

Where is Kwara State University located?

KWASU's main campus is located in Malete, Kwara State, Nigeria. It also has Osi and Ilesa-Barusa campuses.

Before 2007, the state did not have enough institutions of higher learning. A lot of effort had been put towards establishing KWASU, but not much had been achieved for many years.

The turning point came in 2007 when the then governor, Bukola Saraki, constituted a committee that began establishing the university.

Is KWASU a private university?

KWASU is a public institution of higher learning that was established in 2009. It offers quality undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programmes in various fields.

It has seven faculties, 44 departments, and over 7,400 graduates. Over 153,750 students have enrolled in the institution since it was formed.

How to calculate Kwara State University cut off mark?

  • Divide the JAMB score by 8 e.g 320÷8=40
  • Divide the Post UTME by 4 e.g 72÷2=36
  • Add both your JAMB and Post UTME score together: 40+36= 76

Admission into Kwara State University

Admission into KWASU is based on merit, and the institution does not discriminate against any student. All applicants are expected to possess minimum O’level, UTME, POST-UTME, or other stated entry requirements.

Applicants seeking admission into KWASU regular based programmes or other programmes should ensure that all information entered is true and correct to the best of their knowledge.

To apply for any of the programmes listed above, you must first visit the Kwara University website to get the stipulated entry requirements. Then, click on the Admission tab, and select whether you want to pursue an undergraduate or postgraduate programme. You will be automatically redirected to the student portal to start applying for admission.

How to apply online

  • Visit the Kwara State University portal on your device and click Create an Account.
  • Complete the registration form by providing the required information as indicated and click Register.
  • Once you create an account or register with Kwara State University, you will be automatically logged in.
  • The system will automatically send an SMS and email notification to your email and phone number to communicate your application details and login credentials.
  • Your username is the email address you entered at the point of registration, and your surname (last name) is your password.
  • A unique application number will be assigned to you when you have completed the registration. The application number should be used as the username, while your surname (last name) is maintained as the password. You can change this password later.

How much is Kwara State University school fees?

The fee charged differs depending on the course and level of study. Once you successfully apply for a programme and get admitted, the relevant authorities will include a prospectus containing the exact amount you should pay.

Kindly note that all payments are made with an ATM card, and the university is not responsible for any wrong payments. Therefore, you should ensure you read the instructions very well before making any payment.
