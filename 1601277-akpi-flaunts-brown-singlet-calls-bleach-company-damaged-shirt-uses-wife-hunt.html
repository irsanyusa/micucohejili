Akpi Flaunts Brown Singlet, Calls Out Bleach Company Over Damaged Shirt He Uses for Wife Hunt

  • Nigerian singer Speed Darlington has narrated his ordeal with a popular bleach company in the country 
  • The controversial act revealed that he wanted to remove a lipstick stain from one of his favourite shirts he uses to hunt for his wife
  • After soaking the shirt with two of his singlets, the comedian rushed online to share with netizens the strange condition in which he met his clothing

Nigerian singer Darlington Okoye, popularly known as Speed Darlington or Akpi, recently lamented the damage to one of his favourite shirts, which he uses in his quest to find a wife.

The singer, who has been actively searching for a soulmate, explained that he soaked the shirt in a popular bleach to remove a lipstick stain but was shocked by the outcome.

Akpi revealed that he intended to clean his white shirt but was surprised to find it in pieces when he retrieved it from the water.
