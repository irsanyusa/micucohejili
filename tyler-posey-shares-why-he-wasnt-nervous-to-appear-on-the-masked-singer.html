During Wednesday’s new episode of The Masked Singer Season 10, Hawk was unmasked as Teen Wolf actor Tyler Posey. The star, who recently got married, said he wasn’t nervous to perform on the talent show.

Tyler Posey Talks About The Masked Singer

Posey was unmasked during Tuesday’s Harry Potter Night, after singing “Every Little Thing She Does Is Magic” by the Police. The actor told Entertainment Tonight that his time competing on the show was “really fun.”

“I thought I was gonna be nervous, but that never happened,” Posey shared. “I think that’s because I love performing so much. I love giving it my all.”

Posey went on to say that he “felt unstoppable” whenever he had the Hawk costume on, adding, “This was an experience unlike anything else I’ve ever done.”

The actor has experience performing music. He’s been in a few different bands over the years, most recently the pop-punk band Five North. They released an EP called Scumbag in 2020. In recent years, Posey has also released solo music.

Posey Got Married Earlier This Month

Earlier this month, Posey tied the knot with fellow musician Phem. The ceremony was in Pacific Palisades, with a reception at Duke’s Malibu. The actor told Entertainment Tonight that married life has been “incredible.”

“It was one of the best days of my life,” Posey said about the wedding. “And we just have so much fun with each other. It’s really cool, man.”

The couple has collaborated on music in the past, as Phem wrote on his song “Shut Up,” which featured Travis Barker. Posey told the outlet that his wife is “an amazing artist and writer,” adding that “she’s wanted to put out a song with me. So we’re looking forward to doing that.”
