Mark Ballas Posts a Heartwarming Video How He Found Out His Wife BC Jean Was Pregnant

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Getty Images for Nickelodeon

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Dancing With The Stars alum Mark Ballas and his wife, BC Jean, recently shared a heartwarming video on Instagram, documenting the beautiful moment they found out they were going to be parents. In an intimate look at their lives, the couple captured the joy and surprise of discovering they were expecting their first child. Ballas’ ecstatic reaction, paired with Jean’s thoughtful reveal, created a truly unforgettable moment that left their fans and friends overwhelmed with happiness.

Mark Ballas Shared How He Found Out He Was Going to Be a Dad

The video begins with Jean engaging in a sweet conversation with their dog, Hendrix, playfully revealing that she had just learned something exciting. At that moment, she and Hendrix were the only ones in on the big secret.

The camera then pans to Ballas, who is standing in the kitchen, holding his phone. As he reads the handkerchief tied around Hendrix’s neck, with the words “Big Brother” written on it, Ballas’ face lights up with joy. Jean then hands him two pregnancy tests that confirm the wonderful news.

In the caption accompanying the Instagram post, Ballas expresses his overwhelming happiness at becoming a dad. He describes the moment as one of the best in his life and the life they share together. He eagerly looks forward to meeting their little one, expressing his excitement with the hashtag #dad.

The video captures the genuine emotions of surprise, joy, and love shared between the couple as they prepare to embrace parenthood. It’s a beautiful glimpse into their personal journey, and both fans and friends were quick to shower them with love and congratulations in the comments.

Mark Ballas Met His Wife Through Music

Ballas and Jean are a musical duo, known as Alexander Jean, and they have been making new music together. Their creative endeavors have led to some magical moments, including the announcement of their pregnancy through this heartwarming video.

The couple’s love for music brought them together, as they first bonded at a singer-songwriter night at a friend’s house. Jean, known for penning Beyoncé’s hit song “If I Were a Boy,” impressed Ballas with her musical talents. Their love story flourished, leading to their engagement in 2015 and their wedding the following year.

Since departing from DWTS, Ballas has been focusing on his music career alongside Jean. They share a passion for creating music together, and their journey as a musical duo has also been one filled with love and creativity.

Now, with the joyous news of their impending parenthood, Ballas and Jean are embarking on a new chapter in their lives—one that promises to be filled with even more love, happiness, and precious moments. As they eagerly await the arrival of their little one, fans from all around the world send their heartfelt congratulations and best wishes to the soon-to-be parents.
