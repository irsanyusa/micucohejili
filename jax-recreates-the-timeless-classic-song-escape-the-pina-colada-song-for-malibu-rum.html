Jax, the rising pop star known for her catchy tunes and infectious energy, has teamed up with Malibu Rum to release a remix of the classic song “Escape (The Piña Colada Song).” The new version of the song is a perfect fit for the summer, with its upbeat tempo and sunny lyrics. Jax’s vocals are smooth and sultry, and she perfectly captures the feeling of summertime fun and relaxation.

Jax Recreates ‘Escape (The Piña Colada Song)’ For Malibu Rum

Originally introduced to the world in 1979 by Rupert Holmes, “Escape (The Piña Colada Song)” has long been a melodic symbol of carefree relaxation and spirited adventure. Now, with Jax’s contribution, Malibu eagerly anticipates amplifying the song’s impact by integrating it into a vibrant, modern-day remix.

The music video for the remix is just as fun and carefree as the song itself. It features Jax and a group of friends enjoying a day at the beach, swimming, sunbathing, and drinking Malibu Rum Piña Coladas. The video is full of bright colors and positive energy, and it will definitely put you in a summer mood.

Jax’s remix of “Escape (The Piña Colada Song)” is the perfect soundtrack for your next summer get-together. Whether you’re lounging by the pool, taking a boat ride, or just hanging out with friends, this song will get you in the mood to celebrate the season.

In addition to the remix, Jax and Malibu Rum have also partnered to create a limited-edition Piña Colada Kit. The kit includes everything you need to make your own Malibu Piña Coladas, including a bottle of Malibu Rum, pineapple juice, coconut cream, and a recipe card. The kit is the perfect way to enjoy Jax’s remix of the song and celebrate the summer with your friends.

She Talks About Her Music, Upcoming Tour with Big Time Rush

In a recent interview with The Knockturnal, Jax opened up about her upcoming tour with Big Time Rush, her empowering music, and her songwriting process. Jax is no stranger to success. In the interview, Jax talked about how her music is inspired by her own experiences.

“As I got older and started to write songs that were authentic to my own story and my trauma. It connected with a community that has gone through the same struggles that I have.” Jax said “I want my music to be a safe space for people to feel heard and understood. I want them to know that they are not alone and that they are worthy of love and happiness.”

In addition to talking about her music, Jax also discussed her upcoming tour with Big Time Rush. She said that she is excited to tour with the band and meet her fans in person. She is also looking forward to performing her new songs on stage. The tour will be kicking off in July and will visit cities across the United States.
